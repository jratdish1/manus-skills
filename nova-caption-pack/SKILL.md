# Nova Caption Pack

Generate platform-specific captions for a single Nova Bootcamp video.

## Output Format
Provide three separate captions tailored to the algorithm of each platform:

### 1. YouTube Shorts
- Focus: Searchability, series continuity, and long-term retention.
- Include: Question for comments, series episode number, 3-5 broad tags.

### 2. TikTok
- Focus: Fast hook, trending format, raw authenticity.
- Include: Short punchy text, 5-8 trending/niche tags.

### 3. Instagram Reels
- Focus: Relatability, sharing, aesthetic appeal.
- Include: Slightly longer storytelling caption, spacing, 10-15 tags.
